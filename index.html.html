Seaside dining

Matosinhos

Just 10 minutes from Porto city centre, Matosinhos is Portugal's seafood capital. The fish comes straight from the Atlantic, grilled over charcoal right outside the restaurants.

📍 ~10 min from Porto · Atlantic seafood
🐙

Restaurante Gavina

One of Matosinhos' most beloved seafood restaurants. Famous for grilled fish, octopus salad and fresh seafood rice.

SeafoodLocal favourite
🦐

O Lusitano

A true local classic — generous portions of grilled sardines, sea bass and shellfish at honest prices. Always busy at lunch.

Grilled fishGood value
🦀

Churrasqueira Alecrim

Charcoal-grilled fish right on Rua Heróis de França — the famous "restaurant street" of Matosinhos. Pick your fish fresh.

Fresh catchTraditional
🌊

Sea Me (Matosinhos)

Contemporary take on Portuguese seafood. Creative dishes, excellent wine list and a modern atmosphere.

ContemporarySeafood

🐟 Best time to visit: Come for lunch on a weekday to avoid queues. Walk along Rua Heróis de França and pick the restaurant with the most locals — that's always a good sign. Don't leave without trying percebes (barnacles) if they're available!

Vila Nova de Gaia

Port Wine Cellars

Just across the Douro river from Porto, Vila Nova de Gaia is home to the world's finest Port wine cellars. Take a guided tour, taste the wines and enjoy breathtaking views of the river.

📍 Vila Nova de Gaia · Across Ponte Luís I
🍾

Graham's 1890

Est. 1820

Perched on the hillside with one of the best views over Porto. Superb cellar tours and a fantastic restaurant on site.

Cellar tour + tastingRestaurantPanoramic views
🏆

Taylor's

Est. 1692

One of the oldest and most prestigious Port wine houses. Stunning hilltop location, elegant tasting rooms and exceptional Vintage Ports.

Guided tourWine barHistoric cellars

Sandeman

Est. 1790

The most iconic cellar in Gaia, recognised worldwide by its caped Don logo. Excellent guided tours and interactive museum.

MuseumMultiple tastingsGreat for beginners

Ramos Pinto

Est. 1880

Known for its art nouveau posters and beautiful museum. A more intimate experience with excellent Ruby and Tawny Ports.

Art museumIntimate tourRiverside location

🍷 Local tip: Book your cellar tour online in advance — the best slots fill up quickly. After the tour, walk the Gaia riverside and enjoy a glass of Port with views of Porto — magical at sunset.
