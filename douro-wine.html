Douro and Porto wine

The Douro, the wine story and Porto's emblematic viewpoints.

This page combines Douro context, Port wine culture and a few essential central Porto places that connect naturally with the river and the city's identity.

🌊

Douro River

The Douro shapes the identity of Porto. A walk along Ribeira or Gaia makes visitors understand the city more quickly than any guidebook paragraph.

🏺

Port Wine Museum

The Museu do Vinho do Porto helps visitors connect the river trade, the city and the Port wine story before or after a cellar visit.
